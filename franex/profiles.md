# Franex Global Referral - Target Profiles

## Profile A: "The E2 Investor"
- **Nationality:** From treaty countries (82 eligible)
- **Net worth:** $150K+ liquid
- **Goal:** Own US business + live in USA
- **Pain point:** Wants visa without $500K EB5 investment
- **Where to find:** LinkedIn, entrepreneur forums, international business groups

## Profile B: "The EB3 Caregiver"
- **Background:** Nursing assistant, caregiver experience
- **Goal:** Green card through employment
- **Pain point:** No IELTS required (key selling point!)
- **Where to find:** Healthcare job boards, Philippines, India, Caribbean nursing schools

## Profile C: "The Franchise Buyer"
- **Capital:** $25K-$500K
- **Goal:** Business ownership (passive or active)
- **Pain point:** Overwhelmed by 800+ options, needs guidance
- **Where to find:** Franchise marketplaces, business-for-sale sites, LinkedIn entrepreneurs

## Profile D: "The Portugal Seeker"
- **Profile:** Retiree or remote worker with stable income
- **Goal:** EU residency, Schengen access
- **Pain point:** Wants affordable European option
- **Where to find:** Expat forums, digital nomad communities, retirement planning groups
