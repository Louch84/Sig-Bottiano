# Short Squeeze Trading System

## Complete Workflow: First Step to Last

---

## STEP 1: SCAN UNIVERSE

Apply these base filters:
- Price: $0.50 - $50.00
- Float: Under 100M shares
- Volume: 500K+ daily average

---

## STEP 2: SHORT INTEREST SCREEN

Filter:
- Short Interest: Over 20% of float
- Days to Cover: Over 5 days
- Cost to Borrow: Rising over 50%

**Points:** Up to 25 points

---

## STEP 3: SMART MONEY CHECK

Check:
- 13F institutional buying (last quarter)
- Dark pool net positive (5 days)
- Call/Put ratio over 2:1
- Unusual options sweeps over $500K

**Points:** Up to 20 points

---

## STEP 4: TECHNICAL ANALYSIS

Check:
- Consolidation: 10+ days tight range
- Volume: Declining during consolidation
- RSI: Between 50-65
- Price: Above 20-day EMA

**Points:** Up to 15 points

---

## STEP 5: CALCULATE SCORE

| Factor | Max Points |
|--------|-----------|
| Short Interest | 25 |
| Days to Cover | 20 |
| Cost to Borrow | 20 |
| Smart Money | 20 |
| Technical | 15 |
| **TOTAL** | **100** |

---

## STEP 6: DECISION

| Score | Action |
|-------|--------|
| 80-100 | HIGH CONVICTION - Enter position |
| 70-79 | WATCHLIST - Monitor for trigger |
| 60-69 | MONITOR - Need catalyst |
| Below 60 | PASS |

---

## STEP 7: TRIGGER ENTRY

Wait for BOTH:
1. Break above consolidation high
2. 2x average volume on breakout

---

## STEP 8: POSITION MANAGEMENT

**Entry:** On breakout confirmation

**Stop Loss:** Below consolidation low

**Risk:** 1-2% max per trade

**Target:** 
- Next resistance level, OR
- 2:1 reward to risk ratio
