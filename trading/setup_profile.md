# GAP FILL + MOMENTUM SETUP PROFILE

## Variables that make a setup fire:

### 1. Price Action
- Daily gain >3%
- Gap up OR gap fill happening
- Price in $1-$20 range (for cheap options)

### 2. Volume
- Volume 1.5x+ average
- Unusual activity

### 3. Options
- Cheap premiums (<$0.10)
- High open interest
- ITM/ATM strikes

### 4. Technical
- Gap fill pattern
- Breakout above resistance
- High relative volume

### 5. Social/Influencer
- Twitter mentions
- Influencer calls (SniperAlert, etc)
- High engagement

### 6. Short Interest
- Elevated short float (>15%)
- Days to cover increasing

### 7. Sector
- REITs
- Biotech
- Meme stocks
- Recent IPOs

## Universe to Scan
- REITs: MPT, NLY, AGNC, RITM, STC
- Biotech: MRNA, NVAX, VXTR, ATER
- Meme: GME, AMC, KOSS
- Tech: SOUN, AI, PLTR, APP, ARM
- Recent: SMCI, INTI

## Scoring
- Gap: 30 pts
- Daily >5%: 25 pts
- Volume 2x+: 20 pts
- Short >20%: 15 pts
- Price $1-20: 10 pts
