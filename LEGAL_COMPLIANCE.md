# Legal Compliance for Trading Tools

## ⚠️ DISCLAIMER REQUIREMENTS

### Required Disclaimers (Implemented):

1. **NOT FINANCIAL ADVICE**
   - Clear statement that content is educational only
   - Not a substitute for professional advice
   - User must consult licensed professionals

2. **NOT AN INVESTMENT ADVISOR**
   - Not registered with SEC/FINRA
   - No advisory license (Series 65/66)
   - No fiduciary duty

3. **RISK WARNINGS**
   - Options trading involves substantial risk
   - Can lose 100% of investment
   - Past performance ≠ future results
   - Do your own research (DYOR)

4. **LIABILITY WAIVER**
   - User assumes all responsibility
   - Hold harmless from losses
   - No guarantee of accuracy

---

## SEC/FINRA Guidelines

### What Constitutes Investment Advice:
- Specific recommendations to buy/sell securities
- Tailored advice to individual circumstances
- Ongoing monitoring/adjustment of portfolios
- Holding oneself out as an advisor

### What is EDUCATIONAL (Safe Harbor):
- General information about securities
- Educational analysis/methods
- Sharing research/data
- Not tailored to specific clients
- With proper disclaimers

## Best Practices Followed:

1. **Never Say:**
   - "Buy this stock"
   - "You should invest in..."
   - "This is a guaranteed winner"
   - "I recommend..."

2. **Always Say:**
   - "This is for educational purposes"
   - "Do your own research"
   - "Not financial advice"
   - "Consult a professional"
   - "You can lose 100%"

3. **Present As:**
   - Data analysis
   - Pattern recognition
   - Educational examples
   - Informational tools
