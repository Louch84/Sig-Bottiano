# Real Estate Wholesaling - How to Get Deals & Close

## The Model

```
You find deals → Get under contract → Assign to investor → Get paid
```

**Your fee:** $2,500 - $10,000 per deal

---

## How to Find Deals (Ranked by Effectiveness)

### 1. Driving for Dollars 🚗
**Best for: No money, immediate results**
- Drive around distressed neighborhoods
- Look for: boarded up, overgrown yard, abandoned, "For Sale by Owner" signs
- Knock on door or leave flyer
- **Cost:** Gas + flyers (~$50/deal)

### 2. Direct Mail 📬
**Best for: Volume, systematized**
- Send postcards to:
  - Pre-foreclosure owners
  - Absentee owners (don't live there)
  - Tax delinquent properties
  - Inherited properties
- **Cost:** $0.50-$1.00 per mailer
- **Tool:** BatchData, PropertyScout, DealMachine

### 3. Cold Calling 📞
**Best for: Motivated sellers**
- Call owners from skip-traced lists
- Scripts ready
- **Cost:** Skip-trace ($0.10/name) + phone

### 4. Sheriff/Tax Sales 🔨
**Best for: Deep discounts**
- Bid4Assets.com
- Philadelphia Sheriff Sales
- Tax lien auctions
- **Risk:** Higher - can't see inside

### 5. MLS (Foreclosures) 🏠
**Best for: Legit deals, financed**
- Search: REO, HUD, VA foreclosed
- Agents have access
- **Cost:** Agent commission

### 6. Bandit Signs 🚏
**Best for: Leads**
- Put signs: "We Buy Houses"
- Areas with high foreclosure
- **Cost:** $20/sign

### 7. Facebook/Social Media 📱
**Best for: Modern buyers**
- Post in local groups
- "Cash buyer looking for deals"
- **Cost:** Free

## How to Close the Deal

### Step 1: Find the Property
- Driving for dollars
- Direct response marketing
- Networking

### Step 2: Analyze the Numbers
```
ARV (After Repair Value):    $100,000
- Repair Costs:              -$30,000
- Holding Costs:            -$5,000  
- Selling Costs:            -$10,000
- Your Fee:                 -$5,000
= MAX OFFER:                $50,000
```

### Step 3: Get Under Contract
- Explain: "We buy houses, here's how it works"
- Offer: "I'll put it under contract for $50K, then find a buyer"
- Contract: Use state-specific assignment contract

### Step 4: Market to Investors
- List to your buyer list
- BiggerPockets
- Facebook groups
- Direct to investors you know

### Step 5: Assign the Contract
- Assign to investor for $5,000+
- Get paid at closing

## The Funnel

```
Leads → Follow Up → View → Negotiate → Contract → Assign → Get Paid
```

- Call/text within 5 minutes
- 3 touches minimum

## Scripts

### Driving for Dollars Knock
"Hey, I'm walking around the neighborhood and noticed your place. I'm a local investor - we buy houses in any condition. Are you thinking about selling?"

### Seller Call
"I can pay [MAX OFFER] cash. We'd close in 7-10 days. You don't pay any fees. How does that sound?"

### Investor Pitch
"I've got a property at [ADDRESS]. ARV: $100K, needs $25K work. Looking for $55K. Want me to send details?"

## Keys to Success

1. **Lead volume** - More doors knocked = more deals
2. **Fast follow up** - 5 minutes or lose
3. **Know your numbers** - ARV - Repairs - Fees = Offer
4. **Build buyer list** - Sell before you buy
5. **Don't get emotional** - Business decision

---

## Common Mistakes

1. **No buyer list** - Can't assign deals
2. **Wrong numbers** - Overpaying
3. **No marketing budget** - Need consistent leads
4. **Not following up** - 80% of sales happen after 5th touch
5. **Fear of rejection** - Part of the game
