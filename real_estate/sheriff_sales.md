## Due Diligence Checklist (MUST DO)

### 1. Title Check
- [ ] Check liens at Prothonotary's Office (215-686-3565)
- [ ] Check Recorder of Deeds for ownership
- [ ] Check mortgage liens
- [ ] Check judgment liens
- [ ] Check federal tax liens

### 2. Property Info
- [ ] Get property ID/parcel number
- [ ] Check OPA (Office of Property Assessment) for value
- [ ] Check recent sale history
- [ ] Check zoning

### 3. Physical Condition
- [ ] Drive-by / physically visit
- [ ] Check neighborhood
- [ ] Check for occupied vs vacant
- [ ] Note exterior condition

### 4. Financials
- [ ] Calculate: Purchase price + back taxes + fees + renovation = Total in
- [ ] Compare to ARV (After Repair Value)
- [ ] Estimate rent potential
- [ ] Calculate ROI

### 5. Legal
- [ ] Confirm no owners living there (squatters)
- [ ] Check if occupied - Sheriff sales don't give possession
- [ ] Check for bankruptcy (stays with property)

---

## Red Flags (RUN AWAY)

1. **Federal tax lien** - Can't be cleared
2. **Occupied property** - Can't get possession easily
3. **Bankruptcy** - Property still belongs to bank
4. **Massive liens** - More than property worth
5. **Code violations** - City can put lean on property

---

## Good Signs

1. **Vacant** - Easier to take possession
2. **Low liens** - Just property taxes owed
3. **Good neighborhood** - Easier to sell/rent
4. **Structure intact** - Less renovation needed
