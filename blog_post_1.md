## Spring Shopping Strategy

The secret to affordable fashion isn't buying more — it's buying smarter. Here's the formula:

1. **Start with neutrals** (black, white, navy, beige)
2. **Add one statement piece** per season
3. **Ensure everything works with 3+ other items**
4. **Quality over quantity** (fabric matters more than brand)
5. **Fit is everything** (even $10 items look expensive when they fit right)

## Spring Fashion Mistakes to Avoid

**Don't:**
- Buy trendy colors that don't match your existing wardrobe
- Choose quantity over quality (3 cheap shirts vs 1 good one)
- Ignore fabric content (polyester doesn't breathe)
- Buy without trying the "3 ways to wear it" test

**Do:**
- Check the care label (machine washable = you'll actually wear it)
- Consider cost-per-wear ($50 worn 50 times = $1 per wear)
- Buy for your real life, not your fantasy life
- Invest in pieces that solve problems (comfort, versatility, easy care)
