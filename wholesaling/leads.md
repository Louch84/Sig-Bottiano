# Real Estate Wholesaling - Motivated Sellers

## Target Leads

| Source | Platform | Method |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Distressed Properties | Zillow, Trulia | Pre-foreclosure, underwater |
| FSBO | For Sale By Owner | Direct mail, texting |
| Probate | County records | Inheritance sales |
| Divorce | Attorneys | Referrals |
| Job transfer | LinkedIn, FB | Relocating |
| Code violations | City records | Vacant/neglected |

## Lead Sources (Free)
- Zillow/Facebook Marketplace FSBO
- Public records (foreclosure, probate)
- Bandit signs
- Door knocking
- Cold texting (skip tracing)

## Scripts

### Text to FSBO
"Hey, I saw your place on the market. I'm a local investor who buys houses as-is. Not sure if you're looking to sell fast but if you want a cash offer, let me know. No realtor fees."

### Text to Probate
"Hi [NAME], I help families sell inherited properties quickly. If you're looking to sell [PROPERTY ADDRESS], I can make a fair cash offer. No repairs needed. Let me know if you're interested."

### Cold Text
"Hi, I buy houses in [AREA]. Need to sell fast? I pay cash. Call or text [YOUR NUMBER]."

## Tracking
- Lead → Contacted → Made Offer → Under Contract → Sold
- Track: Address, Owner, Asking Price, ARV, Repair Cost, Offer, Profit
