Spring Shopping Strategy

The secret to affordable fashion isn't buying more — it's buying smarter. Here's the formula:

  1. Start with neutrals (black, white, navy, beige)
  2. Add one statement piece per season
  3. Ensure everything works with 3+ other items
  4. Quality over quantity (fabric matters more than brand)
  5. Fit is everything (even $10 items look expensive when they fit right)
