## Trading Philosophy

### Risk Management
- Never risk more than 2% per trade
- Size positions based on account risk
- Always know exit before entry

### Market View
- Trends are friends until they end
- Volatility = opportunity (sell premium)
- Liquidity = survival
- Technicals + fundamentals = confirmation

### Trade Execution
- Wait for setup, don't force trades
- Scale in/out, don't go all-in
- Take profits at predefined levels
- Cut losses fast

5. **Sector rotation** — When big funds shift allocations
